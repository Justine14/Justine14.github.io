Art 321 Week 6 - Radio Play

The Tooth Fairy

Project was completed alongside Komal Waseem, Sabrina Lakhdhir, and Alex Brukalski

Cast: Komal Waseem (Narrator, Intro Person), Sabrina Lakhdhir (The Tootk Fairy aka. Mr. Snookers), Alex Brukalski (Nurse Dirkin), and Justine Bui (Dr. Armadingo) INTRODUCTION SFX *Steps on a sidewalk.* SFX *Door knocking* SFX *Door opens.* Person1: Yes? TF: Hello there. I'm the tooth fairy. And I doth come for ... SFX *Door closing.* Intro music Narrator: This is another sectret adventure of ... the Tooth Fairy. Intro music ends THE SHOW TF: The office of Dr. Nelson C. Armadingo, my official tooth fairy headquarters and place of employment. SFX *Door opens* Dr. Armadingo: Good morning nurse Dirkin! Nurse Dirkin: Good morning Dr. Armadingo. SFX *Door closes* Dr. Armadingo: Aah, which office is Mr. Snookers, my assistant's? Nurse Dirkin: Right over there on the left. Dr. Armadingo: Fine. Nurse Dirkin: But Mr. Snookers .. SFX *Door opens* Dr. Armadingo: (distant) Ah, Mr. Snookers ... Nurse Dirkin: Hasn't come in yet. SFX *Door closes* SFX *Door opens* TF: Good morning nurse Dirkin. Nurse Dirkin: Dr. Armadingo's looking for you. SFX *Door closes* TF: All right, then I'll go see him. SFX *Door opens* Nurse Dirkin: But he's not in his o... TF: (distant) Good morning doctor. SFX *Door closes* Nurse Dirkin: He's in your office. SFX *Door opens* Dr. Armadingo: Nurse Dirkin, Mr. Snookers hasn't come in yet. SFX *Door closes* Nurse Dirkin: Doctor ... Dr. Armadingo: When he does, have him report to the dental lab. SFX *Door opens* Dr. Armadingo: I'll be in here working on my silent cavity remover invention. SFX *Door closes* Nurse Dirkin: Doctor, my Snookers is ... in your office. SFX *Door opens* TF: Nurse Dirkin, Dr. Armadingo isn't even in his office. SFX *Door closes* Nurse Dirkin: I know that, he's in ... TF: Then why didn't you tell me? Nurse Dirkin: I was trying to tell you that he was in YOUR office. Then ... TF: Oh, well that's why I couldn't find him. SFX *Door opens* Nurse Dirkin: But now he's in ... TF: (distant) Good morning Doctor. SFX *Door closes* Nurse Dirkin: The DENTAL LAB. SFX *Door opens* Dr. Armadingo: Ah, Did you call me? Nurse Dirkin: No, I was telling Mr. Snookers ... SFX *Door closes* Dr. Armadingo: You mean he's here? Nurse Dirkin: Yes, he ... Dr. Armadingo: Well, didn't I tell you that I wanted to see him right away? Nurse Dirkin: (pause) I'll send him right in. Dr. Armadingo: And, Nurse Dirkin - Nurse Dirkin: Yes Dr. Armadingo: I'm going to run a test of my new silent tooth decay remover invention. If after I close the door you're able to hear something, buzz me right away and tell me you heard something. SFX *Door opens* Nurse Dirkin: Right Dr. Armadingo: Thank you. SFX *Door closes* SFX *Assorted bangs and crashes* SFX *Buzz of intercom* Dr. Armadingo: (over telephone) Yes? Nurse Dirkin: I heard something. Dr. Armadingo: (over telephone) Thank you. Nurse Dirkin: You're welcome. SFX *Hang up* Narrator: Listen again tomorrow for another secret adventure of the Tooth fairy.
